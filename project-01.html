Luxe Brand Identity

A complete brand transformation for a luxury fashion house, creating a sophisticated visual identity that captures elegance and modern luxury.

Client
Luxe Fashion House
Services
Brand Strategy, Logo Design, Visual Identity
Year
2024
Timeline
8 Weeks

The Challenge

Luxe Fashion House needed a complete brand refresh to appeal to a younger demographic while maintaining their luxury heritage. Their existing identity felt outdated and didn't reflect their innovative approach to sustainable fashion.

The Solution

We created a modern visual identity system that blends timeless elegance with contemporary design elements. The new brand features a custom wordmark, sophisticated color palette, and a flexible design system that works across all touchpoints.
